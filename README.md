**Advanced Vessel Info (AVI)** is a lightweight utility plugin designed to help you document your space program's progress. It allows you to manage vessel metadata, track flight history, and record payload capabilities directly within the VAB, SPH, and mid Flight.

No more switching to external spreadsheets or trying to remember what a specific craft was designed for three months ago!

[Version 1.7.3]

Features

1. Craft Library

Stay Organized: Sort by folder or create your own custom families to group your rockets and planes.
Quick Search: Find any ship instantly; the ship you are currently building is always highlighted.

2. Flight Log, Milestones & Reliability

Auto-Logging: Every launch is recorded automatically with the date and mission goal.
Link Log To: You can link your current craft's logs to another Rocket. This is useful for saved rockets with subassemblies on them.
Mission Status: Mark your flights as Success, Partially, or Failure.
Reliability Score: The mod calculates a success rate so you know which designs are truly flight-proven.
Milestones: Add custom milestones to your crafts so they have a true history.

3. Payload & Efficiency

Cargo Tracking: Record how much weight a ship can carry to the Mun, Duna, or beyond.
Cost Per Ton: Automatically calculates launch efficiency so you can find your cheapest rockets.
Live Stats: See part count, mass, and total cost in real-time while you build.
